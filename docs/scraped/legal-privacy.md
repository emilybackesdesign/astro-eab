Privacy Statement: emilybackes.design

**Introduction**

emilybackes.design understands the importance of safeguarding your privacy. Our commitment to transparency is outlined in this Privacy Statement, which details how we collect, utilize, and protect your personal information when you visit our website.

**Information We Collect**

**Information You Provide:** When you contact us directly (e.g., through our contact form), we may collect information such as your name, email address, and any other details included in your message.

**Usage Data:** We may collect information about how you interact with our website. This includes data like your IP address, browser type, pages visited, the time spent on those pages, and other technical details.

**Use of Information**

Collected information may be used in the following ways:  

**Usage Data:** We may collect information about how you interact with our website. This includes data like your IP address, browser type, pages visited, the time spent on those pages, and other technical details.

**Website Improvement:** Usage data helps us analyze how visitors use our website. This allows us to optimize the site's design, navigation, and content.

**Security:** Technical information may help us identify and prevent security concerns or malicious activity on our website.

**Information Sharing**

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Under limited circumstances, we may share information:  

**Service Providers:** Trusted third-party service providers may have access to your information as needed to perform specific tasks for us (e.g., website hosting, analytics).  

**Legal Obligations:** We may be legally obligated to disclose information if required by law, subpoena, or to protect the rights and safety of ourselves or others.  

**Cookies**

Our website may use cookies, which are small text files stored on your device to enhance your browsing experience. They help us remember your preferences and analyze website usage. You can manage cookies through your browser settings.

**Data Security**

We prioritize the protection of your information. Webflow (our provider) implements reasonable technical and organizational security measures to safeguard your data from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

**Children's Privacy**

Our website is not intended for children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals in this age group.

**Your Rights**

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information. This can include the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your data. Please contact us if you want to exercise these rights.

**Changes to This Privacy StatemenT**

This Privacy Statement may be updated periodically to reflect evolving practices. The "last updated" date at the bottom of this Statement will indicate when revisions were made.

**Contact information**

If you have questions or concerns about our Privacy Statement or data handling practices, please feel free to contact us at **emilybackes.design [at] gmail [.] com**.
